School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The auditorium is like a big theater where big announcements, assemblies, and performances by the choir, orchestra, band, or a proffesional group paid to perform take place. The seats are well-cushioned and quite comfortable. The stage has plenty of space for performances, and the lighting system is also very good.
